WeRoute

An easier way to find the weather AND the traffic conditions driving up to your favorite (or often visited) spots!

If you like to check on the traffic and weather conditions every time you leave the house, WeRoute is the perfect app for you! All you need is to save places you visit often (like school, work, your mom's house?) and with one-two clicks and WeRoute will handle the rest for you. (Why do I sound like an infomercial here?)
