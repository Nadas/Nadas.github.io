Persona Generation for Human Trafficking Victims |

Master's Thesis

As the general purpose of personas is to interpret and synthesize descriptions of user segments, and in light of my research goal, which is to predict and uncover human trafficking incidents, this research suggests that personas can be refined through the analysis of data obtained from social media platforms and other online resources, and be used to identify potential trafficking victims online. The methodology of the suggested solution details the paper’s goal of using data collected from the social network site Twitter along with data provided to us by an organization that helps previous human trafficking to generate personas in automatically.
